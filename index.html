Our Mission

We Built the Roadmap We Wish We'd Had.

ShawToWin was founded on a simple conviction: no aspiring racer should have to navigate motorsport alone, without guidance, without a pathway, and without support. Founder Jamie Shaw learned to race through YouTube at 16, worked multiple jobs to fund his drive, and earned podiums nationally and internationally before building the academy he needed but never had. The result is a structured, affordable three-level curriculum that takes complete beginners to professional-standard competitors, with clear milestones at every stage. This is racing education built from the inside of the sport, not from a boardroom.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know Before You Enroll.

Straight answers on prerequisites, eligibility, and gear before you take the wheel.

01 Do I need prior racing experience to join Level 1?

No. Level 1 is designed specifically for complete beginners. If you can drive and you want to race, Level 1 is your starting point. No track time or kart experience is required before enrollment.

02 What is the age requirement for ShawToWin courses?

We welcome students across a wide age range and offer programs suited to both juniors and adults. Contact us directly and we will confirm the right program for your age group and experience level.

03 How does the Dubai Talent Hunt sponsorship work?

The Dubai Talent Hunt is a worldwide program open to all skill levels. Top performers are sponsored to race in the Sodi World Series Dubai Kartdrome Endurance Championship. ShawToWin handles nominations and supports selected drivers through the process. No prior experience is mandatory to enter.

04 Is equipment provided during training sessions?

Yes. All training karts and essential safety gear are provided during course sessions. You do not need to own equipment to begin. For drivers who want their own gear, our retail and sim distribution arms can help you source the right kit.

05 Can I enroll from anywhere in India?

Yes. ShawToWin offers both on-track modules and sim-based training accessible to students across India. Speak with a coach during your free consultation to confirm the format that fits your location.

06 What happens after I complete Level 3?

Level 3 graduates receive pathway guidance toward competitive racing series and talent programs, including the Dubai Talent Hunt and international selection events. ShawToWin's network and track record mean your progress doesn't stop at the course, it's the start of your competitive career.
